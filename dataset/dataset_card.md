# adaption-legal_clause_simplification

This dataset contains pairs of formal legal or policy clauses and their simplified, plain-language equivalents. The prompts feature complex contractual language regarding payments, confidentiality, and benefits, while the completions provide clear, concise restatements. It is designed for training models to translate legalese into accessible text.

### Dataset size

There are 8,063 data points in this dataset. This is an instruction tuning dataset.
